Profit Per Serve

Recipe Revenue understands as a food based business the role is to purchase ingredients from suppliers and sell a combination of these ingredients at a higher price than what it costs. This skill becomes a relentless pursuit for perfection. Effectively we are a manufacturer, distributor, retailer and a customer relationship manager all under one roof. The benefit of this, is we can control most of the costs. Because our actions are repetitive, the costs can be calculated accurately. Our service on this platform is to improve the time it takes you to calculate your recipe costs. Our goal is to save you $1,000 per recipe in running costs each year, we cannot wait to serve you.

How We Work

In short.. We figure out all the running costs of your food business, then our calculator is able to quicky add these items together and produce a report that tells us a variety of information about how much profit the recipe makes. Once this is known we give you simple tools to compare different recipe combinations so you can optimize your profit per serve.

How much do you charge your customers per meal to cover your rent & property maintainance costs? How much do you pay per year for these costs?

The calculator helps the chef to be more profit conscious by tweaking the preparation & cooking proces this can save you $1000s per year.

Ingredients are the core of your business, your ability to negociate on price has it's limits. Recipe Revenue is the tool your business needs to enable you to optimize the ingredient combinations for higher profit margins.

Features

Each feature of Recipe Revenue was built to optimise your profit margin saving you time & money.

600+ Ingredients

These ingredients are ready for you to use straight away. They are 100% ajustable, you can add more if you would like to at no extra cost

Recipe Comparision

Add multiple versions of the same recipe in one click. Tweaking each recipe will enable a smooth comparision to calculate the most profitable recipe combination

Prep Recipe Integration

This enables you to join recipes together to figure out the total cost of the recipes combined. For example: You make the pasta sauce in prep time but cook the pasta dish when ordered.

Property Costs

Add in your rent, electricity, water, insurance and other similar costs for a more realistic calculation. Using this information will tell you how much you should charge per meal to cover these costs.

Staff Time

Staff can make or break your business. This section of the calculator helps you to make more informed decisions to improve work efficiency and minimise staff costs.

Ingredient Investment

Traditionally ingredients are 30% of the total cost of a recipe.. However if you figured out how to get these costs down to 20%.. the normal 30% estimation wouldn't work. This is why it's important to take this into consideration when calculating recipe profit margins.
